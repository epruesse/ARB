TITLE		AxML / fastdnaML

OCCURRENCE	ARB_NT/Tree/Build Tree from sequence data/AxML + fastdnaML
                ARB_NT/Tree/Add species to existing tree/AxML + fastdnaML

DESCRIPTION     AxML is an accelerated version of fastdnaML.

                - AxML produces the same results as fastdnaML does.

                - AxML is about 35%-47% faster than fastdnaML

                More information in AxML can be found at the homepage
                of Alexandros Stamatakis:

                     http://wwwbode.cs.tum.edu/~stamatak/index.html
